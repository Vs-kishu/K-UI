Ratings

Ratings are used to rate a product or a service. You can create normal star ratings by copying the given classes in your HTML. You can use two variants of Ratings in your project,Star Ratings & Ratings badge. Star ratings provide a visual represantation while Ratings badge provides a numeric rating. Both come in 3 sizes, use following classes for each size -

  • .ratings-sm for small ratings
  • .ratings-md for medium size
  • .ratings-sm for large size

For star ratings, you can use .icon-filled or .icon-half-filled to fill the icon completely or partially.
