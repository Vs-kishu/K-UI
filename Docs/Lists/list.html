Lists

Lists as they indicate themselves, are used to display list of similar items. They can be normal lists with bullet points, without any bullet points or stacked lists as seen in notifications tabs across many social media apps. Generally we need two types of lists, side by side list and a stacked list.
To create a side-by-side list, just create a normal unordered/ordered list and give it a class of d-fx and a gap of your choice.
To create a stacked list, you can use the class fx-col and a gap of your choice.
To give a gap, you can use the utility classes for 4 different sizes

  • .gam-sm for a gap of 1rem
  • .gam-md for a gap of 1.5rem
  • .gam-lg for a gap of 2rem
  • .gam-xlg for a gap of 2.5rem
